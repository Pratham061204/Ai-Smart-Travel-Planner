About Destinaid AI

How Destinaid AI Works

Destinaid uses advanced AI techniques to recommend travel destinations based on your preferences. Our system combines knowledge graphs, informed search algorithms, and machine learning to find the perfect match for you.

AI Techniques Used
Knowledge Graphs

We represent destinations and their features (beaches, culture, climate, etc.) in a graph structure. This allows us to find complex connections between your preferences and potential destinations using graph traversal algorithms.

Graph Theory Network Analysis
Informed Search (A*)

We use A* search to efficiently find destinations that match your preferences. This algorithm uses heuristic functions to estimate how well a destination matches your criteria, allowing us to quickly find the best options.

A* Algorithm Heuristics
Heuristic Functions

Our system uses custom heuristic functions to score destinations based on your preferences. These functions consider feature importance, past trips, and other factors to estimate how well a destination matches what you're looking for.

Scoring Optimization
Recurrent Neural Networks

We use RNNs to analyze sequences of your past trips and predict your future travel preferences. This deep learning approach is particularly effective for modeling sequential data like travel history, capturing patterns in your destination choices over time.

RNN Sequence Learning TensorFlow
Itinerary Generation

Our system generates personalized itineraries based on destination features and your interests. Activities are prioritized and scheduled based on their type, location, and estimated duration.

Planning Scheduling
Visualization

The interactive knowledge graph visualization helps you understand how our AI connects destinations with features. You can explore relationships and see why certain destinations were recommended.
